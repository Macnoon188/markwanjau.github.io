Why Smart People Need Smart & Supportive Managers

Published: May 2025

High-performing individuals often bring creative ideas, unconventional thinking, and technical excellence. But they can also be misunderstood, micromanaged, or sidelined in rigid systems.

As someone in cybersecurity, I’ve learned that leadership isn’t about controlling — it’s about clearing the path. Smart employees thrive under leaders who challenge them, support their learning, and give them room to fail safely.

The future of innovation depends on how well we support and lead talent — especially the unconventional ones.
